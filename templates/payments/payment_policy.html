Refund Policy

Course Purchases

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for course purchases. If you're not satisfied with your learning experience, you can request a full refund within 7 days of purchase, provided you haven't completed more than 30% of the course content.

Certificate Purchases

Certificate purchases are non-refundable once the certificate has been generated. Please ensure you have completed the course requirements before purchasing a certificate.

How to Request a Refund
  1. Contact our support team via email or phone
  2. Provide your order/transaction ID
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
