Frequently Asked Questions

To enroll in a course:

  1. Browse courses on our Courses page
  2. Click on the course you're interested in
  3. Click the "Enroll Now" button
  4. You'll be redirected to a confirmation page once enrolled

After enrolling, you can access the course content from your dashboard.

Once you've enrolled in a course, you can access it in two ways:

  1. Go to your Profile and click on "My Enrolled Courses"
  2. Visit the Courses page and click on "Continue Learning" for any enrolled course

To receive a certificate:

  1. Complete all lessons in a course
  2. Pass any required assessments with the minimum score
  3. On the course completion page, you'll see an option to purchase a certificate
  4. After payment, you can download your certificate immediately

Note that certificates are only available for courses that offer them.

We accept major credit and debit cards including Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. We also support payments through PayPal.

All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to protect your financial information.

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your inbox for a password reset email
  5. Click the link in the email and follow instructions to set a new password
